Koicosmos is a small personal observatory for language, design, learning, and tools for attention.

The first public project here is **Sensus**, an interactive reader for classical languages. It begins with Latin, but the deeper ambition is broader: to make ancient texts easier to enter without flattening their difficulty.

This site will hold essays, notes, project logs, and a public profile that can be found by search engines.
